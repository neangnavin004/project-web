How we grew

Our Journey

2009

The First Squeeze

Founded in a small kitchen in Phnom Penh with just three fruits and a dream to share healthy drinks with the neighborhood.

2013

First Retail Store

Opened our flagship juice bar downtown. Lines out the door on day one — we knew something special was brewing.

2017

Going Organic

Partnered with 20+ certified organic farms, pledging to source 100% natural ingredients across all product lines.

2022

Global Reach

Launched international shipping to 30+ countries. Our cold-pressed goodness is now enjoyed on six continents.

2026 · Now

50,000 Happy Sippers

Celebrating 50K loyal customers and launching our new zero-waste packaging initiative. The best is still to come.
